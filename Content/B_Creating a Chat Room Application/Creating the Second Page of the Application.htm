Creating the Second Page of the Application

The second page of the application will display options for the user to change their name and the room they are chatting in. This page is linked the Settings button that was created in step 5.

 

1. On the top menu, click the+ tab to create Page 2 of the application.

 

2. Under Basic Bricks on the left menu, click Header.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Label Field, type “Settings”.

ii. Click Background Color and type #000000 in the lower right corner of the pop-up box. Click OK.

b. To the left of the brick, click the Arrow.

i. Click Navigate Here and select channel F.

 

3. Under Basic Bricks on the left menu, click Spacer.

a. On the right menu in the Height Field, type “40”.

 

4. Under Basic Bricks on the left menu, click Text.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Text Field, type “Type in the name you want others to see or the room name you want to connect to below.”

ii. On the Font Size Slider, select 13.

iii. Click Text Color and type #000000 in the lower right corner of the pop-up box. Click OK.

 

5. Under Basic Bricks on the left menu, click Spacer.

a. On the right menu in the Height Field, type “40”.

 

6. Under Utility Bricks on the left menu, click Text Input.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Input Name Field, type “User”.

ii. Click Button Color and type #a10022 in the lower right corner of the pop-up box. Click OK.

iii. In the Placeholder Text Field, type “Username”.

b. To the right of the brick, click the Arrow.

i. Click Send Input and select channel C.

 

7. Under Utility Bricks on the left menu, click Text Input.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Input Name Field, type “Room”.

ii. Click Button Color and type #a10022 in the lower right corner of the pop-up box. Click OK.

iii. In the Placeholder Text Field, type “Room Name”.

b. To the right of the brick, click the Arrow.

i. Click Send Input and select channel D.

 

8. Under Basic Bricks on the left menu, click Spacer.

a. On the right menu in the Height Field, type “80”.

 

9. Under Basic Bricks on the left menu, click Button.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Label Field, type “Go Back”.

ii. Click Button Color and type #6d6e71 in the lower right corner of the pop-up box. Click OK.

b. To the right of the brick, click the Arrow.

i. Click Press and select channel E.
