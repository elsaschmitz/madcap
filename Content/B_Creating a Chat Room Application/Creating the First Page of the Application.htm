Creating the First Page of the Application

The first page of the application will display the chat room name, text, and will have a place for users to submit messages. This will be the first page that will display when users access your application.

 

1. Under Add Bricks on the left menu, click All Bricks.

Clicking All Bricks will display all of the brick options the user can choose from.

 

2. Under Basic Bricks on the left menu, click Header.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Label Field, type “Group Chat”

ii. Click Background Color and type #000000 in the lower right corner of the pop-up box. Click OK.

b. To the left of the brick, click the Arrow.

c. Click Navigate Here and select channelRefers to a specfic input or output that is gained from an action performed by a user. E.

 

3. Under Chat Bricks on the left menu, click Chat Room.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Room Field, type “TCOM 404”

b. To the left of the brick, click the Arrow.

i. Click Clear and select channel B.

ii. Click Send Message and select channel A.

iii. Click Set Room and select channel D.

iv. Click Set Username and select channel C.

 

4. Under Utility Bricks on the left menu, click Text Input.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Input Name Field, type “Chat Message”.

ii. In the Button Label Field, type “Send”.

iii. Click Button Color and type #a10022 in the lower right corner of the pop-up box. Click OK.

iv. In the Placeholder Text Field, type “Type a message…”.

b. To the right of the brick, click the Arrow.

i. Click Send Input and select channel A.

 

5. Under Basic Bricks on the left menu, click Button.

a. On the right menu:

i. In the Label Field, type “Settings”.

ii. Click Button Color and type #6d6e71 in the lower right corner of the pop-up box. Click OK.

b. To the right of the brick, click the Arrow.

i. Click Press and select channel F.
